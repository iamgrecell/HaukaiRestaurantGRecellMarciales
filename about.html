About Us

The Haukai Restaurant is situated near the famous Lord of the Rings site in Bay of Islands.

We feature Māori kai and Pākeha kai options in our foods and drinks.

We want the customers to experience Te Ao Māori with the use of karakia for blessing food and other Māori culture elements. The owners are committed in providing the best for the community and the tourists. We believe that the dining experience should leave a memory that lasts and must be extraordinary.

Our goal is to be able to cater to both locals and tourists. We take pride in our service and how we give value in preserving our culture through our foods and dining experiences.
